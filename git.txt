1. Sign In to github.com
 
2. Create a repository by clicking NEW or + button
 
3. Upload created index.html/multimedia files by clicking on "Add File" button
 
4. Click on "Commit Changes"
 
5. For hosting... Click on "Settings" tab
 
6. Click on "Pages" under Code and automation segment in the navigation section
 
7. Select "main" branch and Save
 
8. Wait for sometime to get the URL. Then, click the url to open the page
 
9. Congratulation! You have successfully hosted your first website
